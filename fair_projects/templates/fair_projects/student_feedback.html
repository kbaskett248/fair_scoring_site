Science Fair Feedback and Final Exam

20% of your final exam grade will be the edits you make to your display board based on the feedback you received from the school’s science fair.  These edits and reflections are not optional and must be completed by all students.  The expectation is that all students will “meet the standard” for all sections of the science fair rubric. If you receive a score below 3; you must write a reflection and/or document that your display has been edited.  Your edited boards and accompanying reflections should be submitted to your research teacher non the day of your exam.

Scientific Thought and Process Component and Score (10% Final Exam) - All projects were judged on a 1-4 scale with 1 being low and 4 being exceptional.  For these areas, if your score is a 2 or below you must write a reflection, no shorter than 2 sentences” that details 1) why you were unable to meet expectations (<3), and 2) what changes you need to make so that you do meet expectations in the future. Make sure that each edited section is clearly labeled so that you will receive full credit on your exam.
